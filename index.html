Meet Your Teacher

Passionate About Art,
Dedicated to You

With over a decade of experience in fine arts education, I blend classical technique with expressive freedom. My studio, Adi Fine Arts, is a sanctuary where students discover their unique artistic voice — whether they're picking up a brush for the first time or refining advanced skills.

I hold a Master's in Fine Arts and have exhibited across India. Teaching is not just my profession — it's my calling. Every student brings a world of possibility, and I'm here to help you unlock it.

What We Offer

Our Classes

From foundational drawing to expressive oil painting — we offer structured courses for all levels, delivered both in-studio and online.

✏️ 🟢 Online & Offline
Foundational Drawing

Lines, forms, shading, and perspective — the bedrock of every great artwork. Perfect for beginners and those wanting a solid restart.

Beginner
🎨 🟢 Online & Offline
Watercolour Landscapes

Capture light, atmosphere, and the beauty of nature with transparent, flowing watercolours. Learn wet-on-wet and layering techniques.

Beginner–Intermediate
🖌️ 🔴 Offline Only
Oil & Acrylic Painting

Explore rich textures, blending and impasto techniques with oils and acrylics. Work on canvas with studio-grade materials.

Intermediate–Advanced
🏺 🟢 Online & Offline
Portrait & Figure Study

Master human anatomy, proportions, and the art of capturing emotion. Work from live models and reference photos.

Intermediate
🖊️ 🟢 Online Only
Digital Illustration

Apply traditional art principles to digital tools. Create illustrations, concept art, and digital paintings from home.

All Levels
🧒 🟢 Online & Offline
Kids Creative Art (6–14 yrs)

Fun, structured classes to spark imagination in young minds. Drawing, colouring, collage, and basic painting in a supportive space.
