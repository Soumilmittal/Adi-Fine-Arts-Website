Meet Your Teacher

Passionate About Art,
Dedicated to You

With over a decade of experience in fine arts education, I blend classical technique with expressive freedom. My studio, Adi Fine Arts, is a sanctuary where students discover their unique artistic voice — whether they're picking up a brush for the first time or refining advanced skills.

I hold a Master's in Fine Arts and have exhibited across India. Teaching is not just my profession — it's my calling. Every student brings a world of possibility, and I'm here to help you unlock it.

🎨 MFA Graduate 🖌️ 12+ Years Teaching 🏛️ Exhibited Nationally 💻 Online & Offline
